MÛZA RUBACKYTE
pianist



VIRGINIA POST - 7 Oct. 2002
....Lithuanian pianist Mûza Rubackyté was guest soloist in Franz Liszt's "Piano Concerto No. 1 in E-Flat." With her commanding opening chords, she immediately announced herself as an assertive performer who need not fear being overshadowed by the orchestra.
In fact, if the first movement lacked anything, it was a greater exploration of the light and shade in Liszt's music. Rubackyté seemed more at home in the long singing lines of the slow movement, and she brought a light, sparkling touch to the scherzo like third movement.
The finale was all speed and fireworks, the pianist's tone solid and even a bit heavy at times...
